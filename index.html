RAJASTHAN LUXURY SAFARI

Join us on a luxury journey through Rajasthan for the adventure of a lifetime. Let us guide you to some of the most awe-inspiring cultural and natural landscapes in India. Experience close encounters with regal Bengal tigers in the wild, watch herds of deer roam across golden grasslands, and feel the timeless spirit of the desert flow through your veins..


Our Rajasthan Collection will immerse you in some of the most unique and unforgettable experiences. Begin your journey by enjoying breakfast in a heritage haveli surrounded by peacocks in the gardens. Continue on to Ranthambhore or Jawai to spot leopards and tigers in their natural habitat. Witness a traditional Rajasthani folk performance under a starlit sky as camels wander nearby. Then travel to the Thar Desert to experience the serenity of a luxury desert camp amid endless dunes. As you journey through Rajasthan with us, prepare to be inspired by the people you’ll meet and the stories that unfold around you


We operate a curated collection of luxury retreats and heritage stays across Rajasthan’s most iconic and secluded locations. From palatial residences in Jaipur and Udaipur, to wilderness lodges in Ranthambhore and boutique desert camps near Jaisalmer—we offer it all. In addition, we specialize in creating bespoke, bucket-list adventures across Rajasthan and beyond. So dream big—whether it’s a royal wedding, an epic road trip, or a cultural immersion—we’re here to bring your vision to life. Our passionate and knowledgeable team is ready to welcome you on your unforgettable journey through Rajasthan.

EXPERIENCES IN RAJASTHAN

If you are looking for a luxury escape in Rajasthan, these carefully curated itineraries are designed to inspire and impress. From peaceful desert retreats to exhilarating adventures through ancient forts and palaces, we’ve got it covered. Rajasthan and its surrounding regions offer an endless playground for exploration and discovery. Feel the thrill of camel rides across golden sand dunes at sunset. Experience the wonder of close encounters with vibrant wildlife in Ranthambhore National Park.
