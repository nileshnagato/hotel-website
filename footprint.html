OUR FOOTPRINT

Through our Footprint initiative, we support a diverse range of conservation, health, and education projects across Rajasthan, benefiting local communities, cultural heritage, and the delicate desert ecosystem.

We deeply believe in treading lightly on this ancient land—leaving behind only footprints in the golden sands. Our mission is not only to share the magnificent wonders of Rajasthan, but also to protect them with passion and purpose. From conserving native wildlife in sanctuaries like Ranthambhore and Jawai, to supporting schools and healthcare in remote desert villages, we are committed to responsible, mindful travel. We take our responsibility to minimize resource consumption and environmental impact seriously. By bringing together sustainable tourism, conservation, and community empowerment, we strive to create a balance that allows people and nature to thrive side by side—preserving Rajasthan’s legacy for generations to come.

Conservation

From victims of poaching to predators under threat from human-wildlife conflict, we help protect a diverse range of vulnerable animals and their habitats that need our help to survive.
